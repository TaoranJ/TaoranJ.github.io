About Me

Assistant Professor @ Texas A&M University-Corpus Christi | Former Director of Machine Learning @ Moody’s Analytics | LLM | Predictive Analytics | AI Solutions

Dr. Taoran Ji is an Assistant Professor at Texas A&M University-Corpus Christi, where he focuses on developing innovative AI solutions for complex real-world challenges. He currently leads four active projects funded by multiple sources, supporting research in diverse areas including large language models, time-series forecasting, predictive analytics, financial market analysis, and natural language processing.

Dr. Ji is an active contributor to the academic community, serving as an author, reviewer, and organizer for top-tier machine learning conferences. Prior to his academic career, he was Director of Machine Learning at Moody’s Analytics, where he led efforts in credit risk evaluation and adverse media screening through advanced sequential data modeling.
